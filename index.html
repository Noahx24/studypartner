Core features

Built around how you actually study.

StudyPartner does the part most tools miss — it keeps your plan honest. When life happens, your schedule changes with you.

AI-generated daily schedule

Realistic study plans built from your material and your actual availability — not a generic timer.

Dynamic rescheduling

Miss a day? Fall behind a chapter? Your plan redistributes the workload automatically.

Workload estimation

Upload notes, PDFs, or textbooks. We estimate effort from page count, word density, and complexity.

Burnout prevention

Daily load is capped intelligently. You move forward consistently — without crashing on weekends.

Catch-up mode

Behind on a unit? StudyPartner prioritises the highest-impact material before your exam date.

Lightweight & mobile-first

Low-data, fast-loading, and designed for studying on the bus, between shifts, or on a coffee break.

How it works

From a stack of PDFs to a plan you can actually finish.

  1. 01

    Add your material

    Drop in notes, PDFs, or textbook chapters. StudyPartner reads the structure and counts what matters.

  2. 02

    Tell us your reality

    Hours per day, days per week, exam dates. We work around shifts, lectures, and family time.

  3. 03

    Get a plan you'll keep

    An achievable daily schedule with smart pacing — and revision baked in before exams.

  4. 04

    It adapts. Forever.

    Ahead? Lighter days. Struggling? Slower pace and extra revision. The plan moves with you.
