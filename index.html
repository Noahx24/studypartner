Built for the realities of distance learning

Most planners assume you're a full-time student. You're not.

You're working. You're commuting. You're parenting. Your data costs more than your tuition. StudyPartner is built around that reality, not around an ideal you don't have time for.

  • You don't have four hours a day.

    You've got a shift on Tuesday, a baby on Wednesday, and three free hours on Saturday morning. Most planners pretend that doesn't matter.

    StudyPartner asks how much time you actually have, per day, per week, and only schedules into those hours. Nothing more.

  • Your data is expensive. Apps shouldn't waste it.

    You're not on uncapped fibre. You're on a prepaid bundle that has to last the month. Most edtech assumes you'll just stream another 200 MB. You can't.

    StudyPartner runs on a few MB a day. Moodle metadata syncs in the background; the bytes of a study guide only download when you tick it for AI.

  • You'll miss sessions. Life will happen.

    A normal planner makes you feel guilty about Monday and shows you the same overdue list on Tuesday. The pile gets bigger. You stop opening the app.

    StudyPartner's academic-recovery engine rebuilds your plan overnight. Missed hours get redistributed across the days you have left, weighted by deadline. No guilt, no overdue stack. Just a fresh, finishable plan in the morning.
