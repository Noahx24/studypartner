How it works

From a stack of PDFs to a plan you can actually finish.

Five things StudyPartner does, automatically, every day.

  1. 1

    Ingest your material

    Notes, PDFs, textbooks, past papers, and Moodle content. Upload, sync, or paste.

  2. 2

    Estimate the workload

    Page count, word count, and content complexity tell us how long each chapter really takes.

  3. 3

    Match your availability

    You say "5 hours a day, 4 days a week." We plan around shifts, family, and commutes.

  4. 4

    Get a daily schedule

    A realistic plan for today, this week, and right up to the exam.

  5. 5

    It adapts when you fall behind

    Miss a session, fall behind a chapter, or speed up. The plan reshuffles every day.
