About this app

→

StudyPartner turns the mountain of course material into a realistic daily plan. Connect your university's Moodle (myModules), pick the study guides and tutorial letters that matter, and StudyPartner estimates the workload and fits it into the hours you actually have.

It reads your study guides, breaks them into learning units and subtopics, and schedules them around your deadlines. Fall behind? Completed sessions tune a personal pace multiplier and remaining work is rescheduled automatically. Built mobile-first and low-data — material downloads only when you pick it, and your plan works offline.
