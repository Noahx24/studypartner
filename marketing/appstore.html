About StudyPartner

StudyPartner turns the mountain of course material into a realistic daily plan. Connect your university's Moodle (myModules), pick the study guides and tutorial letters that matter, and let StudyPartner estimate the workload and fit it into the hours you actually have.

It reads your study guides, breaks them into learning units and subtopics, and schedules them around your deadlines. Fall behind? Completed sessions tune a personal pace multiplier and the remaining work is rescheduled — no guilt, just a plan that adapts.

Built mobile-first and low-data: material is downloaded only when you choose it, and your plan works offline.
